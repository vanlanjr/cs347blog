Week 2 Blog

Posted on September 16, 2020

This week in class, I expanded my knowledge on the flex box display pattern. Topics that were covered included the lonely child problem and the n-column UI pattern. I also learned how to implement the infamous Holy Grail pattern. I look forward to applying this pattern in future websites. I might look into changing the desgin of my blog page but that has yet to come.

Outside of class, I did a little bit of extra research myself. I am intestered in making my blog look better so I decided to focus my attention on CSS backgrounds. This week, the video I watched is Complex CSS Backgrounds with a Single Property. After watching the video, I learned about the linear-gradient() property. With this, I was able to make the background have multiple different colors as well change the degree. There are a lot of design patterns that can be created using the linear- or repeating-gradient() property. My new background on all the pages is a demonstration with what is possible.
